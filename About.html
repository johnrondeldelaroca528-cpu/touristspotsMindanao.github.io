About Mindanao Tours

Mindanao, the second-largest island in the Philippines, offers travelers a mix of pristine beaches, towering mountains, rich traditions, and warm hospitality. Our tours are designed to immerse you in Mindanao’s natural wonders and cultural treasures — from the peaks of Mount Apo to the white sands of Samal Island.

Highlights

Samal Island

Relax on white sand beaches and snorkel in crystal-clear waters just minutes from Davao City.

Tinuy-an Falls

Known as the "Niagara Falls of the Philippines," this multi-tiered waterfall in Surigao del Sur is a must-see.

Mount Apo

Climb the highest peak in the country and witness panoramic views of Mindanao’s landscapes.
